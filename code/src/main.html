The following pages will visualize the Co2 emissions of Europe in the year 1980, 1990, 2000 and 2010. To illustrate the impact of Co2 emission I have tried to visualize a correlation between the amount of Co2 emission of a country and the average life expectancy from birth of the highest recorded temperature of that year to see if more extreme temperatures will appear over time.

There also is an option to compare two countries on the same three points as in the other page.

The visualizations regarding the temperature of the european countries are made with data of the hottest days of the year in the capital city of that country. Because of that there is a big difference in the actual hottest temperature in the capital according to the rest of the country. That means that the used temperature is not representative for the whole country.
